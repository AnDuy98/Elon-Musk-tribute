How Elon Musk changes the World ?

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1989 - moved to Canada and worked odd jobs including at a farm and a lumber mill
  • 1994 - moved to USA to attend in University of Pennsylvania
  • 1995 - Musk, his brother Kimbal, and Greg Kouri founded Global Link Information Network, later renamed Zip2
  • 2000 - Transfered business from X.com(a former bank) to PayPal
  • 2002 - Founded SpaceX and became the company's CEO and chief engineer
  • 2008 - Musk assumed leadership of the company as CEO and product architect of Tesla.Inc
  • 2015 - SpaceX began development of the Starlink constellation of low-Earth-orbit satellites to provide satellite Internet access
  • 2016 - co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology startup company, with an investment of $100 million
  • 2022 - started shares of Twitter and slowing became CEO, then changed name it to X
